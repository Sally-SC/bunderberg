About Us

Bundaberg Brewed Drinks, established in 1968 by the Fleming family in Bundaberg, Queensland, is dedicated to producing high-quality, naturally brewed beverages that celebrate authentic Australian flavors. Known for its iconic ginger beer, the company combines time-honored brewing methods with carefully sourced ingredients, resulting in rich, distinctive tastes. Each drink is crafted in small batches to ensure the highest quality and freshness, reflecting the vibrant essence of its tropical surroundings. With a commitment to craftsmanship and a passion for innovation, Bundaberg Brewed Drinks continues to delight customers both locally and globally with its range of refreshing, flavorful beverages.

Our family

At Bundaberg Brewed Drinks, we take pride in our family heritage, founded by the Fleming family in 1968. Our commitment to quality and craftsmanship has been passed down through generations, using traditional brewing methods and locally sourced ingredients. This dedication to authenticity shapes our products and fosters a strong connection with our community. As we grow, our family values remain at the heart of everything we do, guiding us in creating refreshing drinks for everyone to enjoy.

Our craft

At Bundaberg Brewed Drinks, our craft is all about blending tradition with passion to create exceptional beverages. We use time-honored brewing methods and locally sourced ingredients, ensuring that each drink is crafted in small batches for the highest quality and flavor. Our dedicated brewers pay meticulous attention to detail, capturing the rich essence of our ingredients and celebrating authentic Australian flavors. This commitment to craftsmanship shines through in every sip, allowing us to deliver unique, refreshing drinks that stand out and elevate any occasion.
